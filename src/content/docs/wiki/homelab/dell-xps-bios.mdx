This is currently serving as my home server, and for such task, I tweaked options on the BIOS to improve the power efficiency and laptop health.

The specifications are as follows:

- CPU: Intel Core i7-8750H
- iGPU: Intel UHD Graphics 630
- GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti Max-Q (4GB GDDR5)
- RAM: 2x 8GB DDR4
- Storage: 1x M.2 NVME SSD 512 GB
- BIOS Version: 1.27.0

The following sections are categorized as they appear in the BIOS.

## System Configuration

### Audio

- Disable Audio
  - Disable Microphone
  - Disable Internal Speaker

### Thunderbolt

- Disable Thunderbolt <Badge text="watchlist" size="small" variant="caution" />

### Keyboard Illumination

- Disabled

### Miscellaneous Devices

- Disable Camera
- Disable SD Card

### Video

- LCD Brightness
  - On Battery: 10%
  - On AC: 10%

### Performance

Intel Turboboost improves CPU performance by allowing it to surpass its base specification. I've disabled it for now, but may re-enable it later.

- Enable C-states
- Disable Intel Turboboost <Badge text="watchlist" size="small" variant="caution" />
- Enable Intel Speedstep
- Enable HyperThreading <Badge text="watchlist" size="small" variant="caution" />

### Power Management

- AC Behaviour
  - Wake on LAN: LAN Only

- Primary Battery Charge Configuration
  - Primarily AC Use
  - Custom Charge start: 50
  - Custom Charge Stop: 70

### Wireless

- Wireless Switch
  - Disable Bluetooth
- Wireless Device Enable
  - Disable Bluetooth
